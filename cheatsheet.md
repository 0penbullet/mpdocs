## Special markup

![](https://i.imgur.com/cBRk75Q.png)

Hỗ trợ những lable sau:

- tip 
- note
- important

Cấu trúc:

```
.. tip::
	Logout and Login again to avoid **Access denied 404 error** when you go to this product configuration.
```
